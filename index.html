About Us

BHP Direct's Home Improvement is an experienced roofing, handymen, and home improvement company located in Bradford, UK.

We offer roofing, siding and many general contractor services in the West Yorkshire county. Our service area includes but is not limited to: West Yorkshire, UK..

We currently hold an A+ rating with the Better Business Bureau and we offer free estimates with no obligation. All quotes are competitively priced, and all work is guaranteed to be completed to exceed satisfaction.

  Eric L BHP Direct is the current owner/operator and has over 25 years of experience in roofing, siding, drywall, painting, windows, decks, gutters, and many other types of repairs.
  Jamie Adam founded BHP Direct Home Improvement 35 years ago in Bradford, UK.

Services
We offer an extensive variety of home improvement services including:

Roof Install, Leak Repair

We service new or existing constructions with roof materials including asphalt composite shingles, shakes, tile, rubber, and metal. We fully correct the source of any roof leak the first time.

Drywall Install, Hole Repair

Installation of drywall for new construction. Professional patch repair or full drywall/Sheetrock replacement to make holes in the wall and ceiling disappear.

Indoor & Outdoor painting

We expertly paint ceilings, walls, trim, cabinets, sheds, decks, bricks, siding, shutters, and more. We fully repair water spots and correct the cause. We also install or remove popcorn ceiling texture.

Windows & Doors

Cracking or peeling around window exteriors, noticeable drafts, or indoor surface condensation are signs that your windows need to be replaced. We offer full service window and door replacement.

Siding & Gutters

Full installation of vinyl and other types of siding materials. We can replace individual panels, pressure wash and even paint your home's siding. We clean and do full maintenance of gutters and downspouts.

Full Kitchen Remodel

Let us lend a hand in accomplishing your dream kitchen! Full kitchen remodeling with installation of cabinets, countertops of all material, flooring, and wall backdrops.
